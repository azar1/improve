Support us

After 60 years during which IFC hosted about 700 Chinese students of excellence for their training and study in Lyon, now the Institute is adopting a new mission: sending young students from Lyon to learn Chinese Language and culture in China. They will be integrated in our university to become partners for special training in different disciplines. The IFC pursues his mission of linking Lyon and Chinese intellectuals to promote scientific, technological and cultural exchanges.

  • 10000€ : one year scholarship
  • 5000€ : one semester scholarship
  • 1000€ : one month scholarship
  • 500€ : a short term training

Your donations can benefit from tax reduction, as IFC is recognized as of public utility.

Job offer: French Teacher Post

  • We recruit a teacher of French language (French Nationality) for classes in a High School in China.

  • Workplace: Jieyang No.1 High School, Jieyang City Guangdong, China

  • Contract period: 1 year (extendable)
  • Salary: local salary + meals and accommodation
  • Contact: Mr. Lin
  • E-mail: insti8@gmail.com
